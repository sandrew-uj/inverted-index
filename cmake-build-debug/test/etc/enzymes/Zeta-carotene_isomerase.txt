   Zeta-carotene isomerase (EC 5.2.1.12, Z-ISO, 15-cis-zeta-carotene
   isomerase) is an enzyme with systematic name
   9,15,9'-tricis-zeta-carotene cis-trans-isomerase. This enzyme
   catalyses the following chemical reaction
          9,15,9'-tricis-zeta-carotene
           9,9'-dicis-zeta-carotene
   This enzyme is involved in carotenoid biosynthesis.
