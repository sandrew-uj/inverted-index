   GTP cyclohydrolase I (GTPCH) (EC 3.5.4.16) is a member of the GTP
   cyclohydrolase family of enzymes. GTPCH is part of the folate and
   biopterin biosynthesis pathways. It is responsible for the hydrolysis
   of guanosine triphosphate (GTP) to form 7,8-dihydroneopterin
   triphosphate (7,8-DHNP-3'-TP, 7,8-NH[2]-3'-TP).
