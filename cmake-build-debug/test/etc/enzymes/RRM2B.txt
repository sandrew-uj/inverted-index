   Ribonucleotide-diphosphate reductase subunit M2 B is an enzyme that in
   humans is encoded by the RRM2B gene. The gene encoding
   the RRM2B protein is located on chromosome 8, at position 8q23.1. The
   gene and its products are also known by designations MTDPS8A, MTDPS8B,
   and p53R2.
