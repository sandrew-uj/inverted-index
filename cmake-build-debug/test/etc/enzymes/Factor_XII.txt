   Coagulation factor XII, also known as Hageman factor, is a plasma
   protein. It is the zymogen form of factor XIIa, an enzyme (EC
   3.4.21.38) of the serine protease (or serine endopeptidase) class. In
   humans, factor XII is encoded by the F12 gene.
