
   Danny and his friend Joe Pearson discover the entrance to a cave in the
   woods near their home. Professor Bulfinch has just invented a portable
   x-ray machine, and he, along with his geologist friend Dr. Tresselt see
   an opportunity to use the device in the cave. The two adults, along
   with Danny, Joe, and Irene, enter the cave on an expedition. They make
   an astonishing discovery, but they encounter a significant problem
   which prevents them from leaving the cave.
