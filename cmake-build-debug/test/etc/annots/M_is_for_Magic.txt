   M Is for Magic is a collection of child-friendly short fiction by Neil
   Gaiman.

   The stories and poems were selected from previously published
   works,^[1] with the exception of "The Witch's Headstone", which is an
   excerpt from the later-published novel, The Graveyard Book. All the
   stories also appeared in Coraline and Other Stories (2007) published by
   Bloomsbury in the UK.
