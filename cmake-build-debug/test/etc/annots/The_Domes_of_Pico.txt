
   To the lunar domes previously photographed in Blast Off at Woomera and
   situated near Mons Pico has been added a cone emitting powerful neutron
   radiation which is causing havoc to the Earth's nuclear power stations.
   The diminutive Chris Godfrey has the job of piloting a British rocket
   to plant a homing beacon next to the cone to enable a strike by
   American rockets carrying Soviet nuclear warheads...
