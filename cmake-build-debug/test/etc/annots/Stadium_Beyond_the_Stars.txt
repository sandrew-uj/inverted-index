
   Steve Frazer, a champion spacesuit racer on Earth's Olympic team, is
   headed to the center of the galaxy with the rest of the Earth team on
   board the Hellas. When they intercept a mysterious derelict spaceship,
   Steve volunteers to investigate. Once on board the ship, he discovers
   evidence of a non-human intelligence that seems to communicate through
   telepathy. Upon his arrival back to the Hellas Steve tells the others
   what he found, but no one believes him. Disqualified from competition
   on false charges, Steve realizes that he has become mixed up in a
   deadly game of interstellar intrigue.
