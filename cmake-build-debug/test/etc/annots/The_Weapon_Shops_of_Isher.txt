   The Weapon Shops of Isher is a science fiction novel by Canadian writer
   A. E. van Vogt, first published in 1951. The novel is a fix-up created
   from three previously published short stories about the Weapon Shops
   and Isher civilization:
     * "The Seesaw" (Analog Science Fiction and Fact, July 1941)
     * "The Weapon Shop" (Analog Science Fiction and Fact, December 1942)
     * "The Weapon Shops of Isher" (Wonder Stories, February 1949)
