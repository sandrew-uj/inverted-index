
   The plot focuses on the homeless character 'Wizard' and his battle with
   a malignant force from his forgotten past.^[3] In order to survive,
   Wizard must rely on his powerful gift of 'Knowing'. This allows him to
   know the truth of things, to receive fortunes and to reveal to people
   the answers to their troubles. Aiding him in his battle for survival is
   the enigmatic 'Cassie' and several other people from the streets.
