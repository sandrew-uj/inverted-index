   River of Blue Fire is a science fiction novel by American writer Tad
   Williams, the second book in his Otherland series. It was originally
   published in 1998, the paperback in 1999.^[1]

   It continues the saga begun in City of Golden Shadow and takes the
   characters through among others a weird Wizard of Oz simulation, a
   Neanderthal world and a cartoon world.
