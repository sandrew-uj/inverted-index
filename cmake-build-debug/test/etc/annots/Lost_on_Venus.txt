   Lost On Venus is a science fantasy novel by American writer Edgar Rice
   Burroughs, the second book in the Venus series (sometimes called the
   "Carson Napier of Venus series"). It was first serialized in the
   magazine Argosy in 1933 and published in book form two years later.
