   The Radix Tetrad is a group of four science fiction books by A. A.
   Attanasio. The first novel, the Nebula Award-nominated Radix, was
   published in 1981, and the last novel, The Last Legends of Earth was
   published in 1989.^[1]^[2]

   All four books of the Tetrad are being re-issued by Phoenix Pick, an
   imprint of Arc Manor Publishers.^[3]
