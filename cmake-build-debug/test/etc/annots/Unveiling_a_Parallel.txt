   Unveiling a Parallel: A Romance is a feminist science fiction and
   utopian novel published in 1893.^[1] The first edition of the book
   attributed authorship to "Two Women of the West." They were Alice
   Ilgenfritz Jones and Ella Merchant, writers who lived in Cedar Rapids,
   Iowa.
