
   Matt Johansson is a gay New York City actor who lands a part in Chapel
   of Ease, a musical written by playwright Ray Parrish. But Ray dies on
   the play's opening night, and Matt, who had befriended Ray, decides to
   visit the chapel on which the play is based in the dramatist's hometown
   in Cloud County. There Matt encounters the mysterious Tufa and becomes
   embroiled in Ray's Tufa heritage.
