
   The author's first novel, it revolves around the protagonist Phaethon
   (full name Phaethon Prime Rhadamanth Humodified (augment) Uncomposed,
   Indepconsciousness, Base Neuroformed, Silver-Gray Manorial Schola, Era
   7043). The novel concerns Phaethon's discovery that parts of his past
   have been edited out of his mind--apparently by himself.
