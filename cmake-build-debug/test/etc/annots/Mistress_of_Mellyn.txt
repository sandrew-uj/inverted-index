
   A young woman, Martha Leigh, is hired as a governess by Connan
   TreMellyn, a widower, for his daughter, Alvean. Martha travels to
   Cornwall and becomes fascinated by her employer and his dead wife.
   While struggling with her increasingly romantic feeling towards Connan
   TreMellyn, Martha also tries to solve the mystery of his wife's death
   in the haunted mansion while trying to avoid death herself.
