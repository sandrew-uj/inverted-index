   Earthseed is a young adult novel by Pamela Sargent, first published in
   1983.^[1] It is set in the unknown future about a group of teenagers
   who live and grow up on Ship, preparing themselves to live on a
   completely new planet.

   Earthseed is the first book in the Seed Trilogy. The sequels are
   Farseed, published in 2007, and Seed Seeker, published in 2010.
