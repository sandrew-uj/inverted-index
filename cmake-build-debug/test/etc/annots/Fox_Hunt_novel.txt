
   Lachlan Fox is a former Royal Australian Navy Special Forces Clearance
   Diver who is living on Christmas Island after being Dishonorably
   discharged after a mission gone wrong in East Timor. When Fox and best
   friend, former Navy pilot Alister Gammaldi, discover an unusual capsule
   whilst diving off Christmas Island little do they know they have
   discovered something that could result in the outbreak of World War
   III.
