
     * In 1987, Tor Books published the gamebook Dzurlord (A Crossroads
       Adventure in the World of Steven Brust's Jhereg). Brust wrote the
       introduction for this book, which introduced readers to the world
       of Dragaera and its inhabitants.
     * Tor also published The Three Musketeers in paperback in 1994. Brust
       introduced the edition, saying that this translation (anonymous,
       originally published in 1888) was his favorite.
     * Brust contributed the introduction for Manna from Heaven. Wildside
       Press published this collection of stories from Roger Zelazny in
       2003.
