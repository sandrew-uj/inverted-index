
   The novel is based on the events that occurred to Agee in 1915 when his
   father went out of town to see his own father, who had suffered a heart
   attack. During the return trip, Agee's father was killed in a car
   accident. The novel provides a portrait of life in Knoxville,
   Tennessee, showing how such a loss affects the young widow, her two
   children, her atheist father and the dead man's alcoholic brother.
