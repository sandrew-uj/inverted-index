
   When Jeff Converse is convicted of a brutal crime he did not commit, he
   sees his bright future slipping away from him. Then, he is suddenly
   whisked away to a secret underground Manhattan society with mysterious,
   twisting tunnels and chambers, where darkness lurks around every
   corner. He sees the eerie underground group of the homeless and the
   mad, and realizes what he has to do. Jeff must now find a way to escape
   alive from this living hell, with only his bare hands and his wit.
