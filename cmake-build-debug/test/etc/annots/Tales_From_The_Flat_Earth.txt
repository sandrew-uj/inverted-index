   Tales From The Flat Earth is a fantasy series by British writer Tanith
   Lee. The novels take inspiration from One Thousand and One Nights and
   are similarly structured as interconnected stories. The series has been
   well received, and Death's Master, the second book in the series, won
   the British Fantasy Award for Best Novel in 1980.
