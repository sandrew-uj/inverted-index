   Ten Silver Coins: The Drylings of Acchora is a young adult novel
   written by Andrew Kooman.^[1] It is a fantasy novel and its protagonist
   is Jill Strong.^[2]^[unreliable source?] Ten Silver Coins was Kooman's
   debut novel.^[3] Kooman wrote the majority of the book in 2004 while
   working in Asia.^[4] It is the first novel in a three-novel series.^[5]
