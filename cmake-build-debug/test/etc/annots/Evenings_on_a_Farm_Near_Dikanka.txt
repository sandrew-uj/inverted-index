
   Evenings on a Farm Near Dikanka is separated into two volumes of four
   stories each:

   CHast' pervaya , Part One
    1. Sorochinskaya yarmarka , Sorochyntsi Fair
    2. Vecher nakanune Ivana Kupala , St.
       John's Eve
    3. Majskaya noch', ili Utoplennica ,
       May Night, or The Drowned Maiden
    4. Propavshaya gramota , The Lost Letter

   CHast' vtoraya , Part Two
    1. Noch' pered Rozhdestvom , Christmas Eve
    2. Strashnaya mest' , A Terrible Vengeance
    3. Ivan Fiodorovich SHpon'ka i ego tiotushka [Ivan Fyodorovich
       Shpon'ka i yevo tyotushka], Ivan Fyodorovich Shponka and His Aunt
    4. Zakoldovannoe mesto , A Bewitched Place
