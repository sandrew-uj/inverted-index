   Thunder on the Left is a novel by Christopher Morley, originally
   published in 1925. In it, Morley looks at maturity, individual growth,
   and human nature. It was adapted as a play by Jean Ferguson Black in
   1934.

   Movie rights in perpetuity were sold to Picture Entertainment
   International, Lee Caplin, for $52,000 in the 1990s.
