
   The book follows U.S. Army Major Reuben Malich and U.S. Army Captain
   Bartholomew Coleman, both former Special Forces officers, as America
   falls into a civil war after the assassinations of both the American
   President and Vice President. A radical leftist army calling itself the
   Progressive Restoration takes over New York City and declares itself
   the rightful government of the United States.^[1]
