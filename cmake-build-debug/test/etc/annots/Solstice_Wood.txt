
   As a bookseller in California, Sylva Lynn has a comfortable life away
   from her family. But after receiving word that her grandfather has
   died, she reluctantly returns to New York for the funeral. When the old
   magic protecting their house from the fay fails, Sylva's cousin is
   kidnapped and replaced with a changeling. Like her relative Rois
   Melior, the hero of Winter Rose, it is only Sylva, who is part fairy
   herself, who is able to cross the border into the other realm to rescue
   him and return peace to their ancestral home
