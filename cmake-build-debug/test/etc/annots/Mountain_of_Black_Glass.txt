   Mountain of Black Glass is a science fiction novel by American writer
   Tad Williams, the third book in his Otherland series. It was first
   published in 1999 with a paperback edition in 2000.^[1]

   Continuing from River of Blue Fire it brings the characters together at
   the battle of Troy and finally to the heart of the Grail Brotherhood.
