   The Earthsea Cycle, also known simply as Earthsea, is a series of high
   fantasy books written by the American writer Ursula K. Le Guin.
   Beginning with A Wizard of Earthsea (1968), The Tombs of Atuan, (1970)
   and The Farthest Shore (1972), the series was later supplemented by
   Tehanu (1990), and Tales from Earthsea and The Other Wind (both 2001).
   In 2018, all the novels and short stories were published as The Books
   of Earthsea: The Complete Illustrated Edition, with illustrations by
   Charles Vess.
