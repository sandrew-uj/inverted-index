   Light is a science fiction novel by M. John Harrison published in 2002.
   It received the James Tiptree, Jr. Award^[1] and a BSFA nomination^[2]
   in 2002, and was shortlisted for the Arthur C. Clarke Award in
   2003.^[3] The Guardian ranked Light #91 in its list of 100 Best Books
   of the 21st Century.^[4]
